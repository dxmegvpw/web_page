藤井保憲, 超重力理論入門. 産業図書, 2005.

超重力理論の本ですが、非常に基本的なところから書かれています。私の知る限り、多脚場やカルーザ・クラインの理論がちゃんと載っている入門書はこの本くらいではないのでしょうか。超重力についての内容はかなり薄い気がしますが、超対称性やKK理論の話の入門としては、読んでおく価値があると思います。

論文・レビュー

N. Arkani-Hamed, A. G. Cohen, and H. Georgi, Anomalies on Orbifolds, Physics Letters B 516, 395 (2001), arXiv:hep-th/0103135. [INSPIRE]

Their work is related to the localized anomalies. When we consider a extra dimension models, the effective fermion fields become non chiral in general since the four dimensional chiral operator \( \gamma^5 \) is imbedded in a higher dimensional Clifford algebra. Thus, its effective theory should be anomaly free. However, there are ways to make its effective field theory chiral. One of them is a orbifold compactification. Their work studies this simplest case, in general a way. They were able to show that anomalies vanish in the bulk and are localized at orbifold fixed points. It was also a great exercise for me in the orbifold field model.
